Dynamics Sales Analytics Dashboard – Explanation
1. Project Overview

The Dynamics Sales Analytics Dashboard provides insights into sales performance by analyzing profit, sales amount, quantity sold, payment modes, product categories, states, and customers.

This dashboard helps businesses track their monthly profits, customer purchases, product performance, and regional sales trends. It enables managers to make better decisions for improving sales and profitability.

2.  Monthly Profit Analysis

This bar chart shows the sum of profit for each month from January to December.

Insight:
Profits are highest in November and January, while May and July show negative or lower profits, indicating possible losses or reduced sales during those months.

The dashboard displays three important KPI cards.

Insight:
These KPIs provide a quick overview of the overall business performance.

9. Dashboard Filters

The dashboard includes interactive filters such as:

State Filter
Allows users to analyze sales data for specific states.

Category Filter
Allows users to focus on specific product categories like clothing or electronics.

These filters help users customize the analysis according to their needs.

Conclusion:
The Dynamics Sales Analytics Dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of sales performance by evaluating profits, revenue, product categories, payment methods, customer contributions, and regional sales trends. It helps businesses identify top-performing products, profitable regions, and valuable customers, enabling better strategic decision-making.
