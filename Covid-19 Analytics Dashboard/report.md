COVID-19 India Analytics Dashboard – Explanation
Overview

The COVID-19 India Analytics Dashboard visualizes COVID-19 data to understand the spread of the pandemic across different states in India. It shows trends related to testing, vaccination, recovery rate, confirmed cases, and mortality using interactive charts and maps.

Key Visualizations

1. State-wise Testing Distribution
This bar chart shows the number of COVID-19 tests conducted in each state. It helps compare testing efforts across different regions.

2. Vaccination Growth Over Time
This area chart displays how vaccination numbers increased over time, showing the progress of the vaccination campaign.

3. Recovery Rate
The pie chart represents the percentage of people who recovered from COVID-19 compared to other outcomes.

4. Daily Testing Trend
This line chart shows how daily testing increased during the pandemic, indicating improved testing capacity.

5. Pandemic Growth Trend
This graph shows the increase in confirmed COVID-19 cases over time, helping analyze the spread of the virus.

6. States with High Mortality
The map highlights states with higher death rates using color intensity, helping identify the most affected regions.

7. Most Affected States
This bar chart displays states with the highest number of confirmed cases.

Conclusion

This dashboard provides a clear overview of COVID-19 trends in India and helps analyze the impact of the pandemic through visual data insights.
