Hospital Data Analysis Dashboard – Explanation
1. Project Overview

The Hospital Data Analysis Dashboard provides insights into hospital patient data, including medical conditions, treatment procedures, costs, patient satisfaction, and readmission rates.
The dashboard helps healthcare administrators analyze hospital performance and improve patient care.

2. Age Distribution by Medical Condition

This bar chart shows the sum of patient ages grouped by medical condition and age groups (19–35, 36–60, 60+).

Insight:
It helps identify which age groups are most affected by specific medical conditions such as heart attack, stroke, or hypertension.

3. Patient Satisfaction by Condition and Outcome

The donut chart represents patient satisfaction scores across different medical conditions.

Insight:
It helps understand how satisfied patients are with treatment outcomes for different diseases.

4. Treatment Cost by Condition

This bar chart shows the total treatment cost for different medical conditions.

Insight:
Conditions like cancer and heart disease have higher treatment costs compared to others.

5.  Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

Two KPI cards display important metrics:

Total Patients: 984

Total Satisfaction Score: 3541

Insight:
These KPIs give a quick overview of hospital patient volume and satisfaction levels.

6.  Readmission Analysis

This chart shows patient outcomes (Recovered or Stable) and whether they were readmitted.

Insight:
It helps hospitals monitor treatment effectiveness and reduce readmission rates.

7.  Length of Stay by Procedure

The horizontal bar chart shows the total length of hospital stay for different medical procedures.

Insight:
Procedures like surgery, chemotherapy, and cardiac treatments require longer hospital stays.

8.  Satisfaction by Condition

The pie chart displays how patient satisfaction is distributed across different medical conditions.

Insight:
It helps hospitals identify areas where patient satisfaction needs improvement.

9.  Dashboard Filters

Filters such as Gender and Medical Condition allow users to analyze the data for specific patient groups.

Conclusion:
This dashboard provides a comprehensive view of hospital operations by analyzing patient demographics, treatment costs, satisfaction levels, and hospital stay duration. It helps healthcare management make better decisions to improve patient care and hospital efficiency.
